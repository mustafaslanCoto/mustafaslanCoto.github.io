Set & $\mathcal{W}$ & Set of hospital wards, indexed by $j$. \\
Set & $T$ & Set of time periods (e.g., days) in the planning horizon, indexed by $t$. \\
Parameter & $c_n^{(j)}$ & Daily cost of scheduling one nurse in ward $j$. \\
Parameter & $N_t$ & Total nurse pool available on day $t$. \\
Parameter & $\lambda$ & Mean rate of hospital-level nurse absences per day. \\
Parameter & $\Lambda_{t}$ & Random variable to impose unplanned absent nurses on day $t$, where $\Lambda_{t} \sim \text{Poisson}(\lambda)$ \\
Decision variable & $n_t^{(j)}$ & Number of nurses scheduled for ward $j$ on day $t$.
