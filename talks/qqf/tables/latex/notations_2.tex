Set & $\Omega^{j}$ & Set of occupancy scenarios for ward $j$. \\
Parameter & $\omega$ & Scenario (random parameter) representing a realization of occupancy uncertainty. \\
Parameter & $\rho_j$ & Number of patients a single nurse can attend to in ward $j$. \\
Parameter & $c_u^{(j)}$ & Associated penalty cost per understaffed patient for ward $j$. \\
Parameter & $s$ & Index for occupancy scenarios, $s\in\mathcal{S}$. \\
Random variable & $O_{j,t}(\omega)$ & Occupied beds in ward $j$ on day $t$ under scenario $\omega\in\Omega^{j}$. \\
Decision variable & $u_{j,t}(\omega)$ & The number of understaffed patients in ward $j$ at time $t$ under scenario $\omega\in\Omega^{j}$. \\
